Who We Are:

Zertte is a FIRST Tech Challenge team based in Almaty, Kazakhstan, made up of students from multiple schools, including the “Academician Sh. Smagulov Specialized Boarding School for Gifted Children.” We are united by curiosity, creativity, and a shared passion for engineering, robotics, and innovation.

Since our formation, we’ve grown not only as a team, but as a movement — building bold ideas, designing powerful systems, and forming real connections with experts, other teams, and our community.

We’ve qualified for international competitions and proudly represented Kazakhstan on the global stage. Our mission goes beyond winning: we aim to make robotics exciting, accessible, and meaningful for the next generation.

Every robot we build and every meeting we attend is powered by one thing: our passion for FTC.

Our Mission

• INSPIRE — We introduce our community to the world of FIRST and ignite interest in robotics through creativity, storytelling, and outreach.

• IMPACT — We build connections, promote STEM, and grow a culture of innovation and gracious professionalism.

• INNOVATE — We continuously learn, experiment, and develop advanced solutions to tackle every challenge on and off the field.

NEWS

June 24, 2025 — Dubai, UAE

Team Zertte is preparing to compete at the Dubai Off-Season FIRST Tech Challenge Tournament, marking our first international event and a major milestone in our team’s journey.

We are excited to represent Kazakhstan on an international stage, and to compete alongside top teams from the MENA region and beyond. This tournament is an opportunity to test our engineering, strategy, and teamwork in a global environment.

Our robot has been redesigned for precision and performance, and we are aiming to showcase strong autonomous consistency, fast cycles, and creative mechanisms.

Beyond the field, we’re also preparing our outreach portfolio, engineering documentation, and presentation — ready to connect, learn, and grow through this international experience.

Stay tuned — Zertte is ready to bring the energy to Dubai!
